Video Intelligence

Sept 2019

Video Intelligence is an efficiency-web project for internal usage. It can transcribe the recorded audio to text and to note any objects in a scene (e.g. a bike). The text and the identified objects are added to the video metadata which can be uploaded to the internal resources website
