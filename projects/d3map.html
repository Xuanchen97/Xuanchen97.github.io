D3.js World Map

Nov 2019

D3.js World Map was designed for people to interact with it and see how the organization made changes in the world.

Design Thinking

Donor expects more transparency on what happens with the funds they entrusted World Vision Canada with. The company's digital team provides the impact data which collect from the communities around the world annually. Our goal was to create a map to show those impact data more visually and display this application in the mall.
