Tools Inventory Management System

Oct 2019

The system was built for the Rwandan community in Africa to manage donated trade tools and workspaces. It allows for online tool sign-out and sign-in, workspace reservation, tool holds, late fee tracking, and customized report generation.

My Role In The Team

I was responsible for the full lifecycle development of the project with an Agile team, including requirement gathering, design, coding, testing, documentation, and implementation.
