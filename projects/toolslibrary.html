Tools Inventory Management System

Oct 2019

The system was built for the Africa Rwanda community to manage several donated trade tools as Rell as manage Rorkspaces. It contains online sign-out and sign-in of tools, reserving workspaces, putting tools on hold, tracking late fees and rental reports .etc.
